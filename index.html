About Chamunda Hardware
Our journey began back in the year 2000 when brothers Amit and Ashish Khandelwal ventured into the world of hardware with a modest 500 sq.ft shop in Jaipur’s Old Aatish Market.
By staying true to our motto of providing the best products at the right price, we soldiered on one day at a time. The positive word of mouth from customers only accelerated our growth and ensured we never had to go looking for clients. The business started gaining traction.
Two decades later, and thanks to our esteemed customers, we moved into a 10,000 sq.ft showroom in New Aatish Market. At our new showroom, we proudly house more than 10,000 products from hundreds of top Indian & International brands.
Over the years, our clientele has evolved from walk-in customers to a host of 5-star hotels, real estate developers, resorts, celebrities and international clients.
Yet, despite the change in our means and scale of business, we stay equally committed to serving our customers as we did back when we started.
